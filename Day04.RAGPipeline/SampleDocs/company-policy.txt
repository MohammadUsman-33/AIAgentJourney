ACME CORPORATION — EMPLOYEE POLICY HANDBOOK

SECTION 1: WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
Employees may request flexible working arrangements with manager approval.
Remote work is available up to 3 days per week for eligible positions.
Overtime must be pre-approved by department managers.

SECTION 2: LEAVE POLICY
Annual leave entitlement is 25 days per calendar year.
Sick leave is 10 days per year with a doctor's certificate required after 3 consecutive days.
Maternity leave is 6 months fully paid. Paternity leave is 4 weeks fully paid.
Unused annual leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days.

SECTION 3: CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must maintain professional behavior in the workplace.
Harassment of any kind will result in immediate disciplinary action.
Confidential company information must never be shared externally.
Employees must declare any conflicts of interest to HR within 30 days.

SECTION 4: TECHNOLOGY USAGE
Company devices must only be used for work-related purposes.
Personal use of company internet is permitted during break times only.
Installing unauthorized software on company devices is strictly prohibited.
All passwords must be at least 12 characters and changed every 90 days.

SECTION 5: PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted twice yearly, in June and December.
Employees will receive written feedback at least 5 working days before the review meeting.
Salary adjustments based on performance reviews take effect the following quarter.
Employees may appeal review outcomes through the HR department within 14 days.

SECTION 6: BENEFITS
Health insurance is provided for all full-time employees and their immediate family.
Employees receive a monthly transport allowance of $150.
A training budget of $2000 per year is available for professional development.
Company pension contribution is 8% of base salary, matched up to 5% by the employee.
